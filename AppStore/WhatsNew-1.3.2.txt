Share Extension
Share content to ClawChat from any app. Found something interesting in Safari or Photos? Tap Share → ClawChat and it goes straight to your conversation.

Connection Stability
Improved WebSocket reconnection logic with generation-based tracking to prevent stale callbacks and race conditions during reconnect.

Streamlined Settings
Simplified the settings view by removing unused usage metrics and displaying the active model as read-only information.

Security
Gateway credentials now use a shared Keychain access group, enabling secure credential sharing between the main app and extensions.
